The St. Karens Academy is nestled in the tranquil surroundings of the Jal Vayu Vihar residential colony in Sector 25 Noida. The school campus is spread over 2 acres and provides a learning environment that is safe and encourages abundant natural light and fresh air.

We are recognized by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and offer curriculum from Nursery to class 12. For classes 11th and 12th the school offers science and commerce streams along with an array of elective subjects.
